## Project Overview

Chicken has long accounted for over 60% of Malaysia's meat supply, with stable and growing demand driven by population growth and food safety awareness. This project combines intelligent farming technology with a complete industrial chain to deliver a competitive, scalable halal broiler chicken supply system.

## Project Scale

- Total land: approximately 280 acres (56 acres commercial use; 40 acres planned for broiler houses)
- 4 modern closed-loop broiler chicken houses
- 1 breeding stock house
- Projected annual output: 1.824 million birds
- Integrated system: breeding stock → hatching → rearing → food processing → cold chain distribution

## Quality Standards

The project adheres to raw-food grade standards with three absolute zero requirements:

| Standard | Requirement |
|---|---|
| Antibiotics | Zero detection |
| Heavy Metals | Zero detection |
| Salmonella | Zero detection |

Full JAKIM halal certification and digital blockchain traceability are integrated throughout the supply chain.

## Intelligent Farming Technology

Fengqi introduces an intelligent environmental control system covering:
- Automatic ventilation, cooling, heating and lighting management
- Real-time health and growth monitoring
- EnzyLoop enzyme-based ecological feed system — improves Omega-3 content and reduces costs
- Smart management integration achieving approximately 15% improvement in egg production rates

## Strategic Value

Beyond meeting local market demand, the project strengthens Malaysia's regional protein supply capability, food safety infrastructure and halal supply chain — contributing to the upgrade of Malaysia's modern agriculture and halal supply system.
