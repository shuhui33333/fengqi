## Overview

Achievers World Sdn. Bhd. is a cultural media brand under Fengqi Group, established in 2020. Initially launched as a magazine, it officially joined the Fengqi Group in 2026 under the leadership of CEO Zhang Jia. The brand's founding principle is to make written records a witness to history and a guardian of culture, leaving behind authentic and valuable historical materials and cultural memories for future generations.

## Three Business Areas

**Industrial Project Marketing & Planning**
Strategic marketing and promotional planning for industrial projects, helping businesses build brand presence and market visibility in Malaysia and Southeast Asia.

**Corporate Consulting & Resource Integration**
Business establishment support, government liaison, local resource matching, and strategic advisory services for enterprises entering the Malaysian market.

**Public Relations, Media & Event Planning**
Large-scale cultural events, business forums, international exchange programmes, and brand promotion activities bridging Chinese and Malaysian business communities.

## Corporate Culture

The company adheres to two core principles:

- **Rigorous and Experienced** — the team consists of professionals with many years of industry experience, demonstrating solid capabilities and efficient organisation in project execution
- **Stable and Win-Win** — providing clients with reliable solutions and strategic support through a pragmatic and stable development approach

## Recognition & Achievements

Achievers World was awarded the **"Malaysia-China Superb Brand Award 2024 — Cultural Heritage Award"** by *Nanyang Siang Pau*, Malaysia's leading Chinese-language media outlet. The brand has also become an official sponsor of the *Nanyang Siang Pau* Golden Eagle Awards for the second consecutive time.

In collaboration with **Xiamen Boyu Film Studio Co., Ltd.**, Achievers World co-produced the *"Going South to Nanyang"* trilogy of films, shot entirely on location in Malaysia. The project has been widely recognised for its contribution to deepening cultural exchange between China and Malaysia.

## Vision & Mission

To build bridges of cooperation between businesses, institutions and governments — promoting cultural exchange and people-to-people interactions between China and Malaysia, and assisting businesses in entering the Southeast Asian market more smoothly.

## Enterprise Support Services

As more Chinese companies establish themselves in Malaysia, Achievers World provides a comprehensive enterprise establishment support system including:

- Policy and regulatory consultation
- Local resource matching
- Communication with business associations and government agencies
- Legal and human resources assistance
- Cross-cultural communication and coordination
- Business activities and brand promotion
