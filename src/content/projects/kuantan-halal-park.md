## Project Overview

> **Land breakdown:** Kuantan-Gebeng zone — 730 acres · Kuantan-Gambang zone — 100 acres · Total: 830 acres

With a global Muslim population exceeding 2 billion and halal market demand continuing to expand, the Kuantan Digital Halal Food Industrial Park (HALAL Industria Hub) is Fengqi's flagship platform in Malaysia — comprising a 730-acre zone in Gebeng and a 100-acre zone in Gambang (830 acres total), creating an integrated cross-border ecosystem integrating industrial park, logistics supply chain, international trade and digital platform into one.

Located in Pahang, adjacent to the Malaysia-China Kuantan Industrial Park (MCKIP) and approximately 10 kilometres from Kuantan Port, the park benefits from direct access to the East Coast Rail Link (ECRL) and proximity to a planned new international airport — giving it a naturally superior multimodal transport position.

## Focus Industries

The park concentrates on four core halal-certified sectors:

- **Halal Food Processing** — from production and packaging through cold-chain distribution
- **Health Products & Nutraceuticals** — functional foods, supplements and wellness products
- **Cosmetics & Personal Care** — JAKIM-certified beauty and personal care manufacturing
- **Pharmaceutical Products** — halal-compliant medicines and health supplements

## Key Competitive Advantages

**JAKIM Certification Fast-Track**
Malaysia's JAKIM is the world's most internationally recognised halal certification authority, accepted in 80+ countries. Through Fengqi's established processes and government relationships, standard certification timelines are compressed from 30+ days to approximately 10–12 days.

**Logistics Efficiency**
The Kuantan Port–Port Klang direct shipping network, combined with ECRL rail connectivity, reduces cross-border customs clearance from approximately 72 hours to approximately 48 hours for participating businesses.

**Trade Policy Advantages**
RCEP and the Malaysia-EU Free Trade Agreement provide park tenants with tariff rates of 0–5% across major export markets — significantly reducing the cost of entering international markets.

**One-Stop Overseas Service**
Beyond industrial space, the park provides digital management, logistics systems, halal certification services, international market channel connections and ESG compliance support — helping businesses solve every challenge from establishment through to market entry.

## Fengqi's Role

Fengqi serves as the overall industrial planner, resource integrator, cross-border market facilitator and operational coordinator — combining logistics, trade and digital systems to reduce the difficulty of going global and enhance market connectivity for every tenant.
