## Project Overview

Indonesia, with a population of over 280 million, is one of Southeast Asia's largest consumer markets and a critical node in regional logistics and supply chain networks. Fengjie Logistics is Fengqi Group's operational logistics brand in Indonesia, progressively building a regional warehousing and distribution network from 2025 onwards.

With four directly operated depots and distribution centres established in Jakarta, Bandung, Surabaya and Semarang, the network now covers approximately 80% of Java Island's logistics demand across its core economic corridor — reaching a combined population of around 145 million people.

## Services

**Integrated Warehousing + Distribution**
Ambient, refrigerated and regional distribution services across Southeast Asia's largest consumer market. Synergy between warehousing and transportation drives a 35% improvement in inventory turnover efficiency.

**Smart Cold Chain Logistics**
Temperature-controlled warehousing and cold chain transport for agricultural produce, food and functional products. Real-time monitoring across all storage and transit stages maintains product loss rates below 8%, compared to an industry average of 15%.

**Cross-Border Supply Chain**
End-to-end supply chain coordination from production origin through warehousing and transport to end-user channels — enabling smoother product flow between China and Southeast Asian markets.

**Digital Logistics Management**
Integrated order, warehouse management system (WMS), transport and data systems for real-time visibility into logistics status, inventory levels and delivery progress.

## For Chinese Enterprises Entering Indonesia

Fengjie Logistics provides customised supply chain solutions including:
- Warehousing and inventory management across Java
- Nighttime restocking for chain restaurants and supermarkets
- Multi-node delivery and centralised shared warehousing
- Cold chain transport and customs clearance support
- Regional distribution cost reduction of approximately 20%

## Expansion Roadmap

Beyond Java, Fengjie Logistics plans to progressively connect additional Southeast Asian logistics nodes — extending the network across Malaysia, Thailand and Vietnam to create a regional backbone connecting China and Southeast Asia's key commercial centres.
