Fengjie Logistics, Fengqi Group's Indonesian logistics subsidiary, has completed the establishment of four directly operated depots and distribution centres across Java Island — located in Jakarta, Bandung, Surabaya and Semarang.

The network covers approximately 80% of Java Island's logistics needs and serves a combined population of around 145 million people, positioning Fengjie as a significant regional warehousing and distribution operator across Indonesia's core economic corridor.

Operations span ambient and cold chain warehousing, urban distribution, trunk transportation and cross-border supply chain services. The cold chain logistics system maintains a product loss rate below 8%, compared to an industry average of 15%, through real-time temperature monitoring across all storage and transit stages.

Partner businesses have reported a 35% improvement in inventory turnover efficiency and approximately 20% reduction in distribution costs through Fengjie's integrated warehousing and shared distribution model.

The group has also introduced customised solutions for chain restaurants, supermarkets and e-commerce companies — including nighttime restocking schedules, multi-node delivery and centralised shared warehousing arrangements.

Looking ahead, Fengjie Logistics plans to progressively extend its network to Malaysia, Thailand and Vietnam, creating a regional logistics backbone connecting China and Southeast Asia's key commercial centres.
